Bilal Majeed

Ph.D. Student

Bilal completed his BEng Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Namal College, Mianwali in 2015 followed by Masters in Electrical Engineering from Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) in 2018. He started his Ph.D. in Computer Sciences at BDS in November 2019. He is working on creating the variants of interrupt controller units (ICUs) WP-7 to test the Automatic Design of Digital Circuits ( ADDC). His research interests include the automation of Digital Circuits and Intelligent Biomedical Diagnostic Devices. Before startung his PhD. he has been working as a lecturer at The University of Faisalabad.
